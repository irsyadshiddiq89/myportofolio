Roblox / Lua

Personal Project

The Overview

Adventure Quest is an immersive RPG experience designed to bridge the gap between casual exploration and hardcore mechanics. The goal was to create a living, breathing world within the Roblox engine that respects player autonomy while guiding them through a complex narrative.

Project Showcase — YouTube

The Problem

Many RPGs on the platform suffer from "simulator fatigue"—repetitive clicking with little reward. The challenge was to engineer a quest system that felt dynamic, where NPC interactions and environmental puzzles required actual logic rather than just grinding stats.

The Solution

I developed a modular quest framework using Lua. This system decouples the UI from the logic, allowing for complex branching dialogues without spaghetti code.

  • Dynamic NPC State: Characters remember previous interactions, altering future dialogue options.
  • Spatial Inventory: A custom-coded grid inventory system that requires inventory management strategy.
  • Reactive Environment: Weather systems that impact gameplay (e.g., rain makes surfaces slippery).

Tech Stack

Lua Roblox Studio Rojo Blender Git

The Outcome

The beta launch attracted over 5,000 unique visits in the first week with an average session duration of 18 minutes—3x higher than the category average.
